What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are designed to think, learn, and solve problems. AI systems analyze data, recognize patterns, and make intelligent decisions that transform the way we live and work.

Core Capabilities

Machine Learning

Algorithms that learn from data and improve automatically.

Deep Learning

Neural networks inspired by the human brain.

Automation

Smart systems that reduce manual effort and increase efficiency.

Data Intelligence

Turning massive data into meaningful insights.
